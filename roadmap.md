# 🔮 Future Plans

LookSphere is continuously evolving. Here is a breakdown of upcoming features and improvements:

### 🔐 Security & Auth
- Password reset and forgot password flow via Email (utilizing Nodemailer)
- Google OAuth integration
- Two-step verification

### 💬 Communication
- Real-time chat system using WebSockets
- Contact Us page & Detailed FAQ page
- Live notification page (including Admin notes to individual users)
- General mail sending functionality

### 🌐 Community & Engagement
- Follow & Unfollow system
- Comprehensive Like, Comment, and Share mechanics for profiles & posts
- Spotlights (Short video feed)
- Background optimistic operations (non-blocking global UI for media uploads & post edits, edit profile)
- Text-based posts feed (similar to Twitter)
- Leaderboard (Top posts by views, top creators, most viewed profiles)
- Save posts functionality (view saved posts in profile)
- Live Activity feed
- Implement post/user deletion functionality (complete removal from DB and Cloudinary)
